Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

01

Overview

UMIKA ("we", "our", "the Service") is an AI-powered virtual assistant operated by ngoclin.h_ at umika.ngoclinh.org. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

By using UMIKA, you agree to the practices described here.

02

Data We Collect

When you create an account or use the Service, we may collect:

  • Account: Email address and hashed password for sign-in, or basic profile info (name, avatar) via Google OAuth
  • Device: IP address and basic browser/device info for security and abuse prevention
  • Content: Chat messages you send, stored to enable chat history features
  • Preferences: Display name, nickname, and preferences you provide in your profile

We do not collect payment info, precise location, or biometric data.

03

How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the Service — authentication, chat history sync
  • To personalize AI responses based on your nickname and preferences
  • To monitor for abuse, spam, or security threats
  • To improve the Service over time

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

04

Third-Party AI Providers

Your chat messages are transmitted to these providers to generate AI responses:

  • Anthropic Claude API — Anthropic Privacy Policy ↗
  • Groq Inference API — Groq Privacy Policy ↗

Only message content is sent for inference — your email or password is never shared with these providers.

05

Data Storage & Retention

  • Account data and chat history are stored in Cloudflare D1, a secure cloud database
  • Sessions use encrypted HTTP-only cookies with a 7-day expiry
  • You can delete your chat history at any time from within the app
  • Account deletion requests: email work@ngoclinh.org — processed within 30 days
06

Cookies

We use a single secure HTTP-only cookie (session) to maintain your login state. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

07

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

Contact us at work@ngoclinh.org to exercise any of these rights.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The effective date above will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.
